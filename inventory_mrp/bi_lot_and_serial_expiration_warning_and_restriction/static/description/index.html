Lot Number Expiry Warning thrive Apps

Lot and Serial Expiration Warning & Restriction thrive App enhances user experience by issuing timely warnings based on the expiration of lots and serials associated with products. This thrive app not only generates alerts when a lot is about to expire but also enforces restrictions by preventing the use of expired lots. Once entry of a lot for a specific product, if the lot is found to be expired, the app promptly generates an error message, ensuring that users are informed and operate by-product expiration guidelines.
