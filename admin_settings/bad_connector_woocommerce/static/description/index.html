Backend Views

Woocommerce backend with required credentials and import functionality.

Woocommerce backend Advanced Configuration tab.

Partner Management

Customers present in Woocommerce.

Imported Partner view.

Product Categories Management

Product categories present in Woocommerce.

Access Woocommerce categories by clicking the "Woocommerce Product Categories" in the backend menu.

Product Attribute Management

Product Attributes with Values present in Woocommerce.

Imported Product Attribute and additionally, you can retrieve updated information of attribute values by clicking "IMPORT ATTRIBUTE VALUE".

Product Tags Management

Product tags present in Woocommerce.

Imported Woocommerce Product Tags view.

Inventory Management

Set the stock quantity for the product.

Clicking the "UPDATE STOCK INVENTORY" button at the product form view level allows for exporting the updated stock quantity of a particular product to WooCommerce. Additionally, at the backend level, the same button can be utilized to update stock quantities in batch.

Tax Management

Tax present in Woocommerce.

Access Woocommerce Taxes by clicking the "WooCommerce Taxes" in the backend menu.

Sale Order Management

Orders present in Woocommerce.

Imported Sale Order view.

In Woocommerce backend, when you enable "Mark Order Completed On Delivery" the "Send Tracking Information" will be visible.If both the boolean are True, the Sale Order will be set to the 'Completed' status in Woocommerce, and it will also send the 'Tracking Information' to the Woocommerce.

After validating the delivery order, the "EXPORT DELIVERY STATUS" button will be visible in the sale order. Clicking this button will update the WooCommerce order status to 'Completed' in both Woocommerce and the Odoo sale order.

Refunds Management

By clicking on the 'EXPORT REFUND' button to seamlessly export the refund information to Woocommerce, we need to enable the 'Refund Quantity With Amount' boolean.

Updated order refund information in Woocommerce.
